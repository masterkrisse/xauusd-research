# XAUUSD Hypothesis Families

## 1. Asia Range to London Expansion
XAUUSD often compresses during Asia and expands during London. A break of Asia range with real displacement may continue if the move is not already exhausted.

## 2. Liquidity Sweep and Reclaim
XAUUSD often sweeps previous day or session highs/lows, then either rejects strongly or continues after reclaim and structure confirmation.

## 3. Trend-Day Pullback Continuation
On directional days, XAUUSD often gives continuation entries after an impulse leg and controlled pullback, especially when the pullback is shallow and structure remains intact.
